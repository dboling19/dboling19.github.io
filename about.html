About Me

I am Daniel Boling, Goshen College 2023 Graduate with a Bachelor's in IT and Business.

Finding a passion for IT development at a young age, I started out with basics of inventory management and robotics. Finding a specific interest in data engineering, I continued finding new avenues for data management skills and developing software for it.

My first language was Lua, using it for basic terminal applications and learning the basics of variables, loops, functions, and general scripting practices. Later I found the obvious similarities of Python an easy level up. My first "professional" position was in college as a student technical assistant (STA). My roles included general helpdesk customer support (resetting drivers, helping users and students with devices, etc.). A year later being promoted to student technician, increasing my responsibilities to campus-wide office support and device repair. In college I was approached by the IT Director for a web/software development opportunity, the language of choice being PHP. I completed the training desired, and moved on to working on other projects within the college where I learned Symfony PHP, Doctrine, Twig, advanced SQL, and more under the IT Manager and Senior Developer. After graduating, my first position outside of college was as a Private IT Contractor for Goshen City, developing the application seen under the "Projects" page.
